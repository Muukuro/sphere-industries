My motivational letter

For the Spring Trip 2014

Why do I want to be a Spring Tripper?

Because it's going to be awesome!

This year, the chosen country is special for me.

Poland has always been of interest to me because of what they endured during the 2nd world war. Warsaw and Krakow both suffered a lot during the war (an understatement, really) and I find it remarkable how well they have rebuild their cities, culture and architecture.

With the trip I’ll experience first-hand the kind and talented people, and the cities too.

I’m really into shooting architectural pictures, so these two beautiful cities are perfect.

Further, I’m very interested in how the design schools in Poland perform compared to Industrial Design Engineering at the TU Delft.

I only have to finish the Bachelor Eind Project when the Spring Trip starts, so I have a lot of free time on my hands. This sounds like a perfect break from my “spare time” and gives me something to see, explore and do.

As a last motivational point: I’m following a course Polish, so visiting the cities and talking to the locals will help me a lot.

Serdecznie pozdrawiam,

Robert
